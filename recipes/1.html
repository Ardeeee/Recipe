Pasta Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 250g pasta
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Boil pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain.
  2. Heat marinara sauce in a saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Add cooked pasta to the saucepan and toss until well coated.
  4. Serve hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese and chopped fresh basil. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
