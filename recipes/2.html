Salad Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mixed greens
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup sliced cucumber
  • 1/4 cup sliced red onion
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinaigrette

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, sliced cucumber, and sliced red onion.
  2. Drizzle balsamic vinaigrette over the salad and toss until evenly coated.
  3. Serve immediately as a refreshing side dish or light meal.
